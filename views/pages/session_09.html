Session 9

We started by taking a computer science warm up quiz on the Brilliant web site.

We then tried out bubble and insertion sorting algorithms. We started with students in order of last name and sorted in birthday month and day of month order. We looked at videos showing examples of sorting.

We then opened some JavaScript projects and looked at a few games. Games in the JavaScript Projects for Kids are Chapter 5: Battleship, Chapter 7: Clock, Chapter 8: PacMan, Chapter 9: Hangman.

Finally, we looked at some other websites with examples of JavaScript.
